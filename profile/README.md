# Presidencia25

> **Apps and AI for civic service and public service at the highest level.**

Presidencia25 is the open-source home of technology initiatives built by and for the Mexican Presidency. We design, develop, and release tools that make government more accessible, transparent, and effective for every citizen.

---

## 🎯 Mission

Build world-class **apps and artificial-intelligence solutions** that:

- Strengthen civic participation and direct communication between government and citizens
- Automate and improve the quality of public services
- Make government data open, interpretable, and actionable
- Raise the standard of digital public infrastructure in Mexico

---

## 🚀 Project areas

### 📱 Civic Apps
Mobile and web applications that connect citizens with public services, allow reporting of issues, track government commitments, and provide transparent access to public information.

### 🤖 AI & Data
Machine-learning models, natural-language processing tools, and data pipelines that help public servants make better decisions and help citizens navigate complex government information.

### 🔗 Open APIs
RESTful and GraphQL APIs that expose official data—budget, public works, social programmes, legal texts—so that civil society, journalists, and developers can build on top of government information.

### 🛡️ Civic Security
Security and privacy tooling that protects citizens' data and ensures that public digital services comply with national and international standards.
